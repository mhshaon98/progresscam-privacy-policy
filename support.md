## Common Questions

**How do I delete my data?**
You can delete individual entries, trackers, or your entire account data directly in the app settings.

**Is my data secure?**
Yes! Your data is stored locally on your device and synced privately through iCloud. We never see your data.

**Can I use Progress Cam on multiple devices?**
Yes! Your data syncs automatically across all your iCloud-connected devices.

**How do I export my data?**
You can view and screenshot all your progress entries in the app. We're working on export features.
